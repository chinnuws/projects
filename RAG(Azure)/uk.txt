# United Kingdom Knowledge Base

## Climate
The United Kingdom has a temperate maritime climate with mild summers and cool, wet winters.

## Countries
The UK consists of four countries:
- England
- Scotland
- Wales
- Northern Ireland

## Geography
The UK is located on the northwestern coast of mainland Europe. It includes the island of Great Britain, the northeastern part of the island of Ireland, and many smaller islands.

### Major Cities
- London (Capital)
- Edinburgh
- Cardiff
- Belfast

### Landforms
- Highlands (Scotland)
- Lowlands (England)
- Valleys (Wales)
- Coastlines and cliffs
