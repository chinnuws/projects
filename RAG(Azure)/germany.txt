Germany is a country located in Central Europe. Its capital city is Berlin.
The official language is German and the currency is Euro (EUR).
Germany is known for its rich history, prominent in arts, philosophy, and sciences.
Key cities include Munich, Hamburg, Frankfurt, and Cologne.
The country is famous for its automotive industry, with brands such as Volkswagen, BMW, and Mercedes-Benz.
Germany is a founding member of the European Union and has a strong economy.
Major rivers include the Rhine, Elbe, and Danube.
Popular foods are bratwurst, sauerkraut, pretzels, and schnitzel.
Germany is home to the Black Forest, Oktoberfest, and numerous castles.
